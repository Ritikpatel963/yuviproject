About The Expert

Dr. Yuvraj
Jadeja

Consultant Fertility & IVF, Gynaecologist Endoscopy Specialist

Dr. Yuvraj Jadeja is a distinguished fertility specialist recognized for his evidence-based approach and dedication to ethical clinical practice. With years of rigorous training and successful outcomes, he specializes in managing complex fertility challenges that require both scientific precision and deep empathy.

His core clinical interests include recurrent implantation failure, thin endometrium, male infertility, poor ovarian reserve, and tailored stimulation protocols like minimal stimulation and natural cycle IVF. Above all, he is known for honest, transparent counseling that empowers patients to make informed decisions.
